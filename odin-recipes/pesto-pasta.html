Pesto Pasta



Description


Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (any type will do)
  • 1 white onion, finely diced
  • 1 courgette
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Sun dried tomatoes
  • 500g panchetta (or smoked bacon), diced
  • 100g pesto
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan, grated
  • Fresh basil
  • Cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente and leave to one side when done
  2. Meanwhile, sautee the bacon for 5 minutes
  3. Add the onion and courgette
  4. Season with the black pepper, avoid using salt
    as the bacon is salty enough
  5. Once the onions and courgettes have softened (usually after around 5 minutes)
    add the diced garlic for 30 seconds and then take the pan off the heat
  6. Mix in the remaining ingredients and pasta
