Pesto Pasta

Description

This dish is simple, quick and light, perfect if you you are in a rush or just fancy something straightforward. The more cheese you add, the better!
Prep time: 5 minutes Cook time: 12 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (any type will do)
  • 1 white onion, finely diced
  • 1 courgette, cubed
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Dried oregano, a large pinch
  • A dozen sun dried tomatoes
  • 500g pancetta (or smoked bacon), diced
  • 100g pesto, or more to taste
  • Mozzarella, hand torn
  • Parmesan, grated
  • Fresh basil, hand torn
  • Cracked black pepper

Method

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente and leave to one side when done
  2. Meanwhile, sauté the pancetta/bacon on a mid-high heat for 5 minutes
  3. Add the onion and courgette and continue to sauté for a further 5 minutes
  4. Season with the black pepper and oregano, avoid using salt as the bacon is salty enough
  5. Once the onions and courgettes have softened (usually after around 5 minutes) add the diced garlic for 30 seconds and then take the pan off the heat
  6. Mix in the remaining ingredients and cooked pasta together
