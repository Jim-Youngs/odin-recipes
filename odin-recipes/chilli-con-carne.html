Chilli con carne



Description


Ingredients

  • 200g basmati rice
  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 white onion, finely diced
  • 1 large carrot, finely diced
  • 1 red pepper, finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 habanero chilli, diced
  • 1/2 can of kidney beans
  • 1/2 tube of tomato puree
  • 1 can of plum tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 pint of beef stock
  • Dried cumin, 2 teaspoons
  • Dried coriander, 1 teaspoon
  • Fresh coriander, 1 teaspoon
  • Cayenne powder, 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cocao powder, 1 teaspoon
  • Worchestershire sauce, 1 tablespoon
  • Brown sugar, 1 teaspoon
  • Sea salt
  • Cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Sautee the onions, carrots and red pepper in 1 tablespoon of oil
    for about 5 mins, until softened
  2. Add the garlic and habanero and sautee for 30 seconds
  3. Add the minced beef and sear until browned
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Add tomato puree and cook for 2 minutes
  6. Stir in the cumin, dried coriander and cayenne
  7. Add the beef stock and bring to the boil, adding the kidney beans
  8. Turn down to simmer and add the Worchestershire, sugar and cocao
  9. After cooking for at least 1 hour on a low heat, add the tomatoes
    and simmer for a further 20 minutes, adjusting the seasoning to taste
