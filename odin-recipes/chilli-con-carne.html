Chilli con carne

Description

Who doesn't like Chilli?! Great if you are hosting and also a good batch meal to make and freeze if not. Serve with rice and/or tortillas and add extra chilli sauce to taste (I like it xxx hot)! If you take the heat too far then a big spoon of sour cream works perfectly.
Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 90 mins

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 1 white onion, finely diced
  • 1 large carrot, finely diced
  • 1 red pepper, finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 habanero chilli, diced
  • 1/2 can of kidney beans
  • 1/2 tube of tomato puree
  • 1 can of plum tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 pint of beef stock
  • Dried cumin, 2 teaspoons
  • Dried coriander, 1 teaspoon
  • Fresh coriander
  • Cayenne powder, 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon
  • Worchestershire sauce, 1 tablespoon
  • Brown sugar, 1 teaspoon
  • Sea salt
  • Cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Sauté the onions, carrots and red pepper in 1 tablespoon of oil for about 5 minutes on a mid-high temp, until softened
  2. Add the garlic and habanero and sauté for 30 seconds
  3. Add the minced beef and sear until browned
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Add tomato puree and cook for 2 minutes
  6. Stir in the cumin, dried coriander and cayenne
  7. Add the beef stock and bring to the boil, adding the kidney beans
  8. Turn down to simmer and add the Worchestershire, sugar and cocoa
  9. After cooking for at least 1 hour on a low heat, add the tomatoes and simmer for a further 20 minutes, adjusting the seasoning to taste
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander
